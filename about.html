Who We Are

Aspire Athletics Club is a community of passionate runners and athletes dedicated to promoting fitness, camaraderie, and personal growth through the sport of athletics. Founded in 2010, our club has grown to become a vibrant hub for individuals of all ages and skill levels who share a common love for running and athletic excellence.

Club History

Established in 2019, Aspire Athlete Club Academy is one of the oldest & most successful Athletics professional club.

2019-2021

SUCCESS FROM THE START

Club's first swimmer was John Harrison. After several years he was widely acclaimed as a natural coach and teacher. Over his 20-year volunteer career, it has been estimated that more than 200,000 children learned to swim at his Swim Club.
